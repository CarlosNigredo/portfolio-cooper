why do i want to learn typeface design


I fell in love with letterforms while studying my bachelor’s degree in graphic design. Back then I attempted to design a pair of fonts, but couldn’t follow that up because I needed to start working, and type design wasn't (maybe it still isn't) a sector well developed in my country (an international career was definitely out of any real possibility).

Now that I'm facing hard times and giving a thought on my life up to now, I realize that my interest in type hasn't ceased. Since I was a young student my dream has been to become a type designer, and I’m finally ready to give it a serious, though humble, try: I’m taking workshops, and I have some self-initiated typefaces in progress (see below). I’m sure that being part of this course would be a tremendous boost for me because I’ve never been in a specialized class about type.
